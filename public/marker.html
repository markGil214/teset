AR Marker for THREEAR

Print this marker or display it on another device to use with the AR scanner

This is the "Hiro" pattern marker - a standard marker used in AR applications

Instructions:

  1. Print this marker at a size of at least 5 x 5 cm (2 x 2 inches) or display it on another device
  2. Ensure the marker is well-lit and placed on a flat surface
  3. Point your device's camera at the marker from about 20-30 cm (8-12 inches) away
  4. Hold your device steady until the 3D model appears
  5. Once detected, you can move your device around to view the 3D model from different angles

Troubleshooting Tips:

  • Lighting: Make sure the marker is in a well-lit area without glare or shadows
  • Distance: Not too close, not too far - find the sweet spot
  • Stability: Keep your hand steady when pointing at the marker
  • Visibility: Ensure the entire marker is visible in the camera view
  • Debug Mode: If available, try enabling debug mode in the AR application
